Bretzel

Fresh and delicious Brezel. Super soft and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 400g spelt flour, 630
  • 21g fresh yeast
  • 7g salt
  • 230ml water
  • 20g olive oil
  • 1 liter water
  • 50g baking soda

Instructions

  1. Put the flour in a mixing bowl, form a small well and crumble the yeast into it.
  2. Mix with 3 tablespoons of warm water 2-3 times and cover with a little flour from the edge.
  3. Cover and let the pre-dough rise for 15 minutes.
  4. After the 15 minutes, add the remaining ingredients to the bowl and knead with the hook for 5 minutes on a medium setting. You could also use your hands but it will take longer.
  5. Cover with a cloth and let rise for 1 hour. You can also let the dough rise in the fridge over night but then only use 10g of yeast.
  6. After rising time, knead the dough well again on a work surface and divide into 4 equal pieces.
  7. Roll the pieces of dough into an approx. 60cm long strand and knot it into a bretzel.
  8. Place the bretzel on a baking sheet lined with baking paper and let rise covered for 30 minutes.
  9. Preheat the oven to 220 Â° C top / bottom heat.
  10. Bring the water to a boil and add the baking soda, then remove from the stove. Mix with a wooden spoon until the baking soda has dissolved.
  11. Place the bretzel one after the other in the lye bath and leave to stand on both sides for 15 seconds.
  12. With the help of a ladle, remove the bretzel from the bath and place them back on the baking sheet.
  13. Sprinkle immediately with salt or sesame seeds
  14. Bake in the oven for about 20 minutes until they have taken on the typical color of the bretzel.
